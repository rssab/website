## Recommended Components

While we finalize our kit offerings, here's what we use and recommend:

### Panels
- **Material:** Graphite Enhanced EPS (GPS/Neopor)
- **Size:** 500√ó330√ó25mm
- **Source:** dekokopf.com (German supplier, ships EU)

### Exciters
- **For PA use:** Xcite XBT40, XT25
- **Budget option:** Dayton Audio DAEX30HESF-4 (4 per panel)
- **Source:** Parts Express (US), SoundImports (EU)

### Amplification
- **Rack amps:** Morin GaN series (excellent efficiency)
- **Plate amps:** Sanway D2S 2CH 900W (budget, proven)
- **Active modules:** ICEpower 200AS2 or Connex IRS2200SMPS

### Processing
- **Pro:** Linea Research DSP
- **Budget:** MiniDSP 2x4 or similar

### Coating
- **Hide glue:** 3:1 water to granules, brushed on
- **Shellac:** Thin layer over glue for sealing
