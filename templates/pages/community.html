Why open source?

Previous attempts to commercialize DML have failed partly because the technology stayed locked behind patents and proprietary implementations. We're taking a different approach.

By making everything open, we let the community validate, improve, and spread the technology. More builders means more experimentation, more feedback, and faster iteration.

The business model is simple: we sell the hard-to-make parts and kits. The knowledge is free.
